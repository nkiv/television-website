Sponge Bob Website

A square yellow sponge named SpongeBob SquarePants lives in a pineapple with his pet snail, Gary, in the city of Bikini Bottom on the floor of the Pacific Ocean. He works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab. The show records the shenanagans that Spongebob and his friends encounter on their adventures during their everyday lives.
